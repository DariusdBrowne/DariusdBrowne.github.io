Insolita Cottages

Welcome to Insolita Cottages, your home away from home. We are a beach front development consisting of well appointed cottages located within walking distance of the famous St.Jame's Club. Situated in Antigua and with a backdrop of the Caribbean sea, Insolita Cottages is distinctive.

Insolita, a Latin word meaning unusual or unique, demonstrates our philosphy. Addopting a unique and distinctive style, everything we do, from out landscaping to our furnishing and finishing, promises to leave you with memorable experiences.

Offering all the comforts of home and supportted by the service of the five-star hotel, Insolita cottages will be a place that you will visit often and be happy to call your place in th sun.

Insolita Cottages tailors its services to meet your needs whether your taste extend to Champagne for Breakfast or to cocktail, drunk to the setting sun, Insolita can provide you a la carte services at an affordable price.
