Nomad Ltd offers a variety of outdoor tours for all types of interests. We have offered tours all over the world and sold sporting equipment for bicycling, hiking and other activities.

These tours are designed for a wide variety of people and interests. You are offered a mix of travel and sightseeing, always with the chance to trek to one of our amazing beaches - 365 of them!

We also offer an opportunity to 'hook-up' with one of our Hiking groups, or just a walk to explore St Johns City. At each stop you can get a taste of our local food and drinks and enjoy a bit of local banter!

Antigua and Barbuda is very sports oriented! The sports enthusiasts can enjoy a game of Cricket, Football(Soccer), Volleyball etc. Many of these will offer you a little on-field play with local participants.
